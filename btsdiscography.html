BTS Discography

Discography of korean boy group bangtan sonyeondan (방탄소년단) 2013-2022



2 Cool 4 Skool

2 Cool 4 Skool is the debut single album by South Korean boy group BTS. It was released on June 13, 2013. The album consists of nine songs, including two hidden tracks, with "No More Dream" as the lead single. The group also promoted another song, "We Are Bulletproof Pt.2", from the album along with the first single.

O!RUL82?

O!RUL8,2? is the first mini-album by South Korean boy group BTS. Released by Big Hit Entertainment on September 11, 2013 shortly after the first single-album 2 Cool 4 Skool. The album consists of ten tracks, with "N.O" as the title song. The group later promotes "Attack On Bangtan", another track from the album.

Dark & Wild

Dark & ​​Wild is the debut studio album by South Korean boy group BTS, released through Big Hit Entertainment on August 19, 2014. It contains 14 tracks, with "Danger" as the title track. The group later promoted "War of Hormone", another title track from the album.
